About ALLTOOLHUB

Built by developers, for everyone. Free tools that just work.

Our Story

ALLTOOLHUB was founded in 2024 with a simple mission — to give everyone access to powerful online tools without paywalls, sign-ups, or ads that get in the way. We believe productivity tools should be free, fast, and accessible to anyone with a browser.

What started as a small collection of image and PDF tools quickly grew into a comprehensive platform with 120+ tools across 11 categories — from image conversion and PDF management to SEO analyzers, developer utilities, and smart calculators.

Today, ALLTOOLHUB serves thousands of users every day — students, designers, developers, content creators, marketers, and everyday people who just need to get things done.

Our Mission

To build the world's most comprehensive, free, and privacy-first online tools platform — where every tool works entirely in your browser, with no file uploads to our servers and no data stored or sold.

Is ALLTOOLHUB really free? ▼
Yes, 100% free. All 120+ tools are completely free with no sign-up, no credit card, no hidden charges. We sustain the platform through advertising revenue.
Do I need to create an account? ▼
No account required for any tool. Just open the tool and start using it immediately.
Are my files safe? Do you store them? ▼
Your files are completely safe. All file processing happens entirely in your browser. Your files never get uploaded to our servers — we have zero access to them.
What data do you collect about me? ▼
We collect anonymous usage analytics via Google Analytics and serve ads via Google AdSense (which uses cookies). We do not collect personally identifiable information unless you contact us.
Why isn't my PDF tool working? ▼
PDF tools like PDF to Text use PDF.js and require an internet connection. For advanced conversions (PDF to Word, PDF to Excel), results may vary as these require complex processing. For critical conversions, we recommend dedicated desktop software.
The Image to Text (OCR) tool is loading slowly. Why? ▼
The OCR tool uses Tesseract.js (~10MB download on first use). After the first load it's cached. Make sure you have a stable internet connection.
How large a file can I upload? ▼
Files up to 50MB work well on most modern devices. For very large files (100MB+), your browser may slow down due to RAM limitations.
Which browsers are supported? ▼
Chrome 90+, Firefox 88+, Edge 90+, Safari 14+. We recommend keeping your browser updated. Internet Explorer is not supported.
Does ALLTOOLHUB work on mobile? ▼
Yes! ALLTOOLHUB is fully responsive. Some tools work better on larger screens. Chrome or Safari on mobile is recommended.
A tool isn't working / I found a bug. What do I do? ▼
Contact us with: the tool name, your browser and OS, what you expected, and what happened. Screenshots are very helpful. We usually fix reported bugs within 48–72 hours.
Why does ALLTOOLHUB show ads? ▼
Ads allow us to keep ALLTOOLHUB completely free for everyone. Without ad revenue, we would need to charge for tools. We keep ads non-intrusive and relevant.
How do I opt out of personalized ads? ▼
Visit Google's Ad Settings (g.co/adsettings) or aboutads.info. You'll still see ads — just non-personalized ones.
Can I request a new tool? ▼
Absolutely! Send us a message via the Contact page with "Feature Request" as the subject. We regularly add tools based on user demand.
Can I use ALLTOOLHUB tools commercially? ▼
Yes! You can use the output of our tools for any purpose including commercial projects. You may not resell or redistribute ALLTOOLHUB's platform itself.
